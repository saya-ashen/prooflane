# Output Templates

## Template A: Standard evidence-first review

```md
# [Project review title]

## Executive summary
[Short, evidence-grounded overview]

## Claim / Evidence / Inference highlights
- Claim:
- Evidence:
- Inference:

## Strengths supported by evidence
- ...

## Risks and limitations
- ...

## Missing information
- ...

## Scores
- Evidence quality:
- Product maturity:
- Technical clarity:
- Maintainability and activity:
- External validation:
- Risk transparency:
- Weighted mean (1-5):
- Provisional total (0-100):
- Evidence coverage (0-1):

## Confidence
[High / medium / low, with why]
```

## Template C: Blind-review result

```md
# Blind Review Result

## Summary
## Evidence-backed strengths
## Evidence-backed risks
## Weakly supported positives
## Provisional score
- Dimension scores (1-5 or uncertain)
- Weighted mean (1-5)
- Provisional total (0-100)
- Evidence coverage (0-1)
## Confidence and uncertainty
```
